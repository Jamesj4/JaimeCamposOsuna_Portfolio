For this Metroidvania-style project, we needed a player character with extremely responsive and fluid movement, as the core gameplay revolves around mobility and precision. I developed a custom 2D controller that supports tight, snappy controls and smooth transitions for a satisfying traversal experience.

In addition to the player movement system, I implemented two types of AI: one for grounded enemies and another for flying creatures. The flying AI uses a custom 2D pathfinding system that allows it to navigate freely around obstacles and dynamically follow the player through environments.
